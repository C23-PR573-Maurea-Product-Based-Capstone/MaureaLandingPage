    Our Journey

    We found that consumers have a hard time determining the maturity level of fruit or vegetables on the market and end up purchasing the wrong goods that can lead to food wastage.

    Therefore, we wanted to develop an application that could help solve this problem and provide a simple, accessible solution for customers to determine the ripeness of fruits and vegetables in the market. With the widespread availability of smartphones and advances in technology, especially machine learning and object detection, we saw an opportunity to leverage these tools to create a user-friendly application that could accurately detect the ripeness of fruits and vegetables in real-time.
